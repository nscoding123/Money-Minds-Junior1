Investing

Imagine you have a special kind of plant. You put a seed in the ground and take care of it. As time goes by, that seed grows into a big, beautiful tree that gives you yummy fruits. Investing is a bit like that!

When you invest, you put your money into something special, like buying a tiny piece of a company or giving money to help a project. Then, just like that seed, your money can grow over time. This happens because the thing you invested in might become more valuable, and you might get more money back than you put in.

Remember: Just like you take care of your plant by giving it water and sunlight, you need to be patient and let your investments grow over time.
