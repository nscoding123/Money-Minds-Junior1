Budgeting

Other slices can be for spending, like getting a new toy or treat. You might even have a slice for sharing or doing something nice for someone else.

When you do this planning with the money you get every month, it is called “Budgeting”.

Fun Activity: Buy 3 jars and label them as “Save”, “Spend” and “Give” jars. After budgeting every month, you can put your money in these separate jars and let it grow till you have the amount you need!
