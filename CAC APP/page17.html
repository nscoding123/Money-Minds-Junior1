Accessing Your Money

There are various means by which you can access your money in your bank account. Lets see the main ways:

Checks: Checks are special pieces of paper that the bank gives you for having a checking account with them. These checks are bound like a flip book.

If you bought a toy from your friend for 3 dollars but dont have the cash to give him, you can write a note to your bank in the check and tell them to give your friend 3 dollars from your account. Your friend can take the check to their bank and get the money.

Did you know: The checks were first used in the United States toward the end of the 17th century.
