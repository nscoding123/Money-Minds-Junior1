Budgeting

Lets imagine that you help your neighbor by walking their dog every day and you get 10 dollars every month for that. We will learn how to budget this.

Think of your monthly earnings as a yummy pizza. Budgeting is like cutting your pizza into slices and deciding what to do with each slice to make sure it lasts until the next time you get more.

Some slices can be for saving, like putting into your piggy bank for something big you want in the future.
