Banking Terms - Types of Accounts

Savings Account: A Savings Account is like having your piggy bank at the bank for money that you dont right away. When you put your money into this for future use, the bank keeps it really safe. They even add a little extra money over time, called "interest," just to keep your money with them. It's like a bonus for being a great money-saver!

Checking Account: A Checking Account is like having your purse at the bank for money that you need for day-to-day use. Most banks do not offer an interest on checking accounts

Remember: You don't actually keep your piggy bank or purse at the bank - you have them in Savings and Checking accounts at the bank in your name.
