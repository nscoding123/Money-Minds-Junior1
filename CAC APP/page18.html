Accessing Your Money - Debit Card

Debit Card:

Imagine you have a magical card that's connected to your bank account. This card is called a "debit card."

When you want to buy something, you can use this card instead of using coins or bills. It's like saying, "Hey, bank, give the store this much money from my account!". When the purchase is complete, the money from your account is ‘debited’.

Did you know: Debit cards made their debut in 1978.
