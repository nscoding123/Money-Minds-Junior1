Introduction to Money

Coins and bills are two different kinds of money that you might see.

Coins are small, round pieces of metal with cool pictures on them. They come in different sizes and colors. People use coins to buy smaller things like candy or stickers.

Bills are pieces of paper that are a little bit bigger than your hand. They have pictures of important people and buildings. People use bills to buy bigger things like toys or clothes. Money is important, but you need to learn how to use it wisely - how to save for later and not spend it all at once.

Remember: Just like you take care of your toys, you should take care of your money too!
