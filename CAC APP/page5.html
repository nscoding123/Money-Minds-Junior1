Earning Money

Coins and bills are two different kinds of money that you might see.

3. Lemonade Stand: On a hot day, you could set up a lemonade stand in your yard. Make yummy lemonade and sell it to people passing by. It's a classic way to earn money and have fun at the same time!

4. Babysitting: If you're a bit older, parents might pay you to look after their children while they're busy. It's a big responsibility, but it can be really rewarding too.

5. Selling Crafts: If you're creative, you could make crafts like friendship bracelets, drawings, or even homemade slime. You can sell these cool creations to people who like them.

Remember: Earning money is a great way to learn about responsibility and the value of hard work. You can use the money you earn to buy things you want or save up for some special goals!
