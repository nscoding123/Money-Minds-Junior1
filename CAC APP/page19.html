Accessing Your Money - ATM

ATMs:

Have you seen those machines where people put in a card, and money comes out? The cards inserted are debit cards and those machines are called ATMs, which stands for "Automated Teller Machines.

Remember: ATMs are like a mini bank machine that helps you take out your money whenever you need it. It's super handy!
