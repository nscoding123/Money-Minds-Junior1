Investing - Benefits

Investing has some great benefits:

Growing Money: Just like your plant grows into a big tree, your money can grow too. Over time, you might get back more money than you originally put in.

More Fun: When your money grows, you can use the extra money to buy more things or save for big goals, like a special toy or a fun trip.

Helping Companies: When you invest in a company, you're helping them do cool things, like making new inventions or providing helpful services. You become a part-owner of the company!

Learning About Money: Investing teaches you about how money works and how to make smart choices with it.
