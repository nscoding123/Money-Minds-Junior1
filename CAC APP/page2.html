Introduction to Money

Let's start with knowing about money.

Imagine you want to have an ice cream. You stand in the line to get it. When your turn comes, you ask for a strawberry double dip in a waffle cone. You give something to the shopkeeper and he gives you your ice cream. Just like magic!

This magical something that got you the ice cream is called “Money” / “Currency”. Each country has its own form of currency.

Did you know: Over 5,000 years ago, people did not use money to get what they wanted. They used to trade something they had to get something they needed, called “barter”.
