Banking Terms - Deposits

Deposits:

Imagine you received 5 dollars from your dad when you helped with dishes. You can now put this money into your bank account so it stays safe. This is called making a "deposit".

Remember: Just like putting your new toy in your toy box, making a deposit is like putting your money where it belongs.
