Needs vs Wants

Needs are things that are super important and basic for us to live happily and stay healthy. Food, Water, and Home are “needs”.

Wants are things that make us happy, but we can live a good life even if we don't have them. Toys, Sweets, and Fancy clothes are “wants”.

Remember: Choosing needs before wants is like making sure you do your homework before you play video games.
