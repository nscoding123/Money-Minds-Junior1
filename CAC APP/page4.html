Earning Money

Well then, how do you get money?

People earn money by doing jobs like going to work, helping others, or even doing chores at home. They then use the money they earn to buy things they want or save it for later.

What are some ways that you can make money?

1. Doing Chores: When you do tasks to help out at home or your friends, like setting the table, walking the pets, or watering the plants, you might get a little bit of money as a reward. It's like a special thank-you for being helpful!

2. Selling Things: Do you have toys, books, or clothes you don't use anymore? You could set up a garage sale where you sell these things to other kids or grown-ups who want them.
